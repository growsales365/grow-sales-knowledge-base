### 🚀 Fractional AI Engineer / Claude Code Expert — Grow Sales

**Location:** Remote (Async-first)
**Type:** Fractional — 10–20 hrs/week | Project-based | Potential to grow full-time
**Compensation:** Competitive, based on experience (+ equity potential discussed)

---

#### About Grow Sales

Grow Sales is an AI agency on a mission to transform how contractors and service businesses operate. We build practical, high-impact AI automation systems that save time, generate leads, and scale operations — without the enterprise price tag. We're a small, fast-moving team shipping real products for real clients. If you like building things that matter, you'll fit right in.

---

#### The Role

We're looking for a sharp, self-directed **Fractional AI Engineer** who lives and breathes **Claude Code**. This isn't a "write prompts and call APIs" role — you'll be architecting and building end-to-end AI systems: automated content pipelines, knowledge bases, SaaS integrations, and more. You'll work async, own your projects, and have a direct line to the founder.

This is a fractional engagement (10–20 hrs/week) with real room to grow. If you're great and the fit is there, this becomes full-time.

---

#### What You'll Build

- **Automated video content pipelines** using Remotion (React-based video rendering) + ElevenLabs TTS + Pexels API + Groq — turning structured data into polished video at scale
- **RAG-powered knowledge bases** for AI department management — indexing SOPs, client data, and playbooks for retrieval-augmented workflows
- **OpenClaw / MasterClaw skills** — custom agent skills that extend our AI assistant infrastructure
- **API integrations** across our stack: GoHighLevel (GHL), Blotato, GitHub, and more
- **White-label SaaS features** for contractors — building systems others can run under their own brand

---

#### What We're Looking For

- **Claude Code fluency** — you know how to prompt, chain, and get real work done with it
- **React / TypeScript** — comfortable enough to read and write Remotion components and API glue code
- **API-first mindset** — you build integrations, not just scripts; you think in webhooks, rate limits, and error handling
- **Async & self-directed** — you can take a brief, clarify what you need, disappear for a few days, and come back with something working
- **Builder DNA** — you've shipped AI projects before, not just played with them

---

#### Bonus Points

- Make.com or n8n workflow automation
- Python (data processing, scripting, LLM pipelines)
- Whisper / Groq for audio/speech workflows
- GoHighLevel (GHL) — CRM automation, sub-accounts, snapshots
- Experience with vector DBs (Pinecone, Supabase pgvector, Chroma)

---

#### Why This Role Is Exciting

- **Cutting-edge stack** — Claude Code, Remotion, ElevenLabs, Groq, OpenClaw. You're not maintaining CRUD apps.
- **Real products shipping** — everything you build goes into live client systems or our own SaaS platform
- **Direct impact** — small team means your work shows up immediately, no bureaucracy
- **Equity potential** — for the right person who grows with us, we're open to equity conversations
- **Grow with it** — fractional now, but this company is scaling fast
