# Grow Sales — Social Media Video Script (Remotion)

*Format: 9:16 vertical | Duration: 60–90 seconds | Platform: TikTok / Reels / YouTube Shorts*

---

## HOOK OPTIONS (Test All Three — Pick Your Best Performer)

---

### Hook A — Pain-Based
**"You're already behind on AI. Here's why."**

[SCENE 1 — 0s–3s]
TEXT: "You're already behind on AI."
SUBTEXT: (smaller) "Here's why."
VISUAL: Black screen → bold white text, sharp cut-in
TONE: Direct, slightly confrontational

---

### Hook B — Cost-Based
**"A senior AI engineer costs $10k/mo. We're not that."**

[SCENE 1 — 0s–3s]
TEXT: "A senior AI engineer costs $10k+/mo."
SUBTEXT: "We're not that."
VISUAL: Dollar sign animation → price tag crossed out
TONE: Bold, disruptive

---

### Hook C — Curiosity-Based
**"Your competitors are automating everything. Are you?"**

[SCENE 1 — 0s–3s]
TEXT: "Your competitors are automating everything."
SUBTEXT: "Are you?"
VISUAL: Split screen — one side manual work, one side automated dashboard
TONE: Urgent, FOMO-driven

---

## MAIN VIDEO — FULL SCRIPT

*Uses Hook A (strongest for cold audiences — leads with pain)*

---

[SCENE 1 — 0s–3s]
TEXT: "You're already behind on AI."
SUBTEXT: "Here's why."
VISUAL: Bold white text, black background. Hard cut.
AUDIO: No music. Silence for impact.

---

[SCENE 2 — 3s–12s]
TEXT: "AI changes every single week."
SUBTEXT: "New models. New tools. New capabilities."
VISUAL: Fast-scroll montage of AI headlines, tool logos flashing — ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, new releases
AUDIO: Low beat starts building
VOICEOVER (optional): "Every week there's something new. A better model. A faster tool. A smarter way to automate."

---

[SCENE 3 — 12s–22s]
TEXT: "Your competitors who ARE keeping up?"
SUBTEXT: "They're getting faster. Cheaper. More automated."
VISUAL: Side-by-side — frustrated business owner doing manual work vs. clean dashboard showing automated pipeline running
AUDIO: Beat intensifies slightly
VOICEOVER (optional): "The businesses investing in AI right now are pulling ahead. Every month the gap gets bigger."

---

[SCENE 4 — 22s–35s]
TEXT: "You could hire a senior AI engineer."
SUBTEXT: "$10,000–$15,000/month. One person. No guarantees."
VISUAL: Job listing mockup for "Senior AI Engineer" — salary highlighted in red
AUDIO: Beat holds steady
VOICEOVER (optional): "You could try to hire someone. But good AI talent is expensive, hard to find, and harder to evaluate."

---

[SCENE 5 — 35s–50s]
TEXT: "Or you could hire Grow Sales."
SUBTEXT: "Your personal AI team. Already built. Already current."
VISUAL: Clean brand moment — Grow Sales logo, team/work visual, dashboard showing automations running
AUDIO: Beat lifts — more energetic
VOICEOVER (optional): "We're a full AI development team. We build your systems. We manage them. We keep them current — every single week. You run your business. We run your AI."

---

[SCENE 6 — 50s–60s]
TEXT: "We've invested $10k's building the best AI systems."
SUBTEXT: "We implement them in your business."
VISUAL: Brief proof visual — system diagram, automation flowchart, or screen recording of AI workflow in action
AUDIO: Beat at peak energy

---

[SCENE 7 — 60s–75s]
TEXT: "DM us 'AI'"
SUBTEXT: "We'll show you exactly what we'd build for your business."
VISUAL: Clean CTA screen — bold text, brand colors, finger pointing / tap animation on DM button
AUDIO: Beat resolves cleanly
VOICEOVER (optional): "DM us the word AI and we'll map out exactly what we'd build for your business. No pitch. No pressure. Just a real look at what's possible."

---

[SCENE 8 — 75s–80s] *(if padding needed)*
TEXT: "@GrowSales"
SUBTEXT: "Your AI Development Partner"
VISUAL: Logo hold / outro card
AUDIO: Fade out

### Platform Recommendations

| Platform | Priority | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **TikTok** | 🔴 High | Best for cold reach, pain-based hooks perform well |
| **Instagram Reels** | 🔴 High | Strong for B2B-adjacent audiences, use Hook B or C |
| **YouTube Shorts** | 🟡 Medium | Longer shelf life, good for curiosity/cost hooks |
| **LinkedIn** (adapted) | 🟡 Medium | Reformat as landscape or square for feed, tone down slightly |
| **Facebook Reels** | 🟢 Test | Lower priority but worth testing for older business owner demo |

### A/B Test Recommendations

- **Week 1:** Launch with Hook A (Pain-based)
- **Week 2:** Test Hook B (Cost-based) on same audience
- **Week 3:** Test Hook C (Curiosity-based)
- **Evaluate:** Watch rate (3s), retention (50%), and DM conversion
- **Winner:** Boost with paid spend on best-performing variant
