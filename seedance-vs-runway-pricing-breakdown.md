# Seedance vs Runway: Pricing Breakdown

## Written Guide

### Why Pricing Matters at Scale

When you're generating AI video clips manually, $1–2 per clip feels fine. When you're automating a content pipeline producing 10–50 videos per day, that cost compounds fast. This guide breaks down exactly what each platform costs, what you get, and how to do the math for your actual use case.

---

### Seedance Pricing

Seedance is a newer entrant with very competitive pricing for volume.

#### Subscription Plans

| Plan | Price | Credits/Month | Est. Clips (1080p) |
|---|---|---|---|
| Free | $0 | Limited | ~3-5 test clips |
| Plus | $14.90/mo | ~100 clips worth | ~100 videos |
| Pro | $24.90/mo | ~250 clips worth | ~250 videos |

> *Note: Seedance structures credits per video/clip, not per second. Plans above reflect approximate clip counts based on 1080p standard duration (5–10 seconds).*

#### Pay-Per-Use
- **1080p clip (5–10 sec):** ~$0.50–$0.75 per clip
- No monthly commitment required
- Good for burst usage or testing before subscribing

#### What You Get
- Text-to-video generation
- Image-to-video (animate a still)
- Consistent character/style prompting
- 1080p output, up to ~10 seconds per clip
- Relatively fast generation (minutes, not hours)

---

### Runway Pricing

Runway (RunwayML) is the more established, feature-rich platform — but pricing reflects that.

#### Subscription Plans

| Plan | Price | Credits/Month | Est. Clips (10-sec, 720p) |
|---|---|---|---|
| Free | $0 | 125 credits | ~3–5 clips |
| Standard | $15/mo | 625 credits | ~12 clips |
| Pro | $35/mo | 2,250 credits | ~45 clips |
| Unlimited | $95/mo | Unlimited (standard quality) | Varies |

#### Credit Cost Per Clip
- **5-second clip at 720p:** ~25 credits (~$0.60)
- **10-second clip at 720p:** ~50 credits (~$1.20)
- **10-second clip at 1080p:** ~100 credits (~$2.40)
- **Upscaling, motion brush, etc.:** additional credits

#### What You Get
- More advanced motion controls than Seedance
- Motion Brush (control which parts of the image move)
- Image-to-video, text-to-video
- Longer clips possible (up to 16 seconds on some plans)
- Stronger cinematic quality ceiling
- More consistent results for complex prompts

---

### The Math: Automation at Scale

#### Scenario 1: Daily Content Creator (1 video/day)
One AI clip per video:
- Seedance Pay-per-use: **$0.50–$0.75/day → ~$15–22/month**
- Seedance Plus: **$14.90/month flat → better deal**
- Runway Standard: **$15/month → ~12 clips, then pay more**

**Winner at this volume: Seedance Plus** (100 clips for $14.90 vs Runway's 12 for $15)

---

#### Scenario 2: High-Volume Pipeline (10 clips/day = ~300/month)
- Seedance Pro: **$24.90/month → 250 clips, then ~$0.50–$0.75 for overages**
- Runway Pro: **$35/month → ~45 clips, then ~$2.40/clip overage**
- Runway Unlimited: **$95/month → better for volume, but 4x Seedance cost**

**Winner at this volume: Seedance Pro** by a wide margin for raw clip count

---

#### Scenario 3: Premium AI Influencer Content (3–5 clips/week)
You want the best quality, not cheapest price:
- Runway Pro for motion quality + control: **$35/month covers it**
- Seedance Pro for consistency + volume: **$24.90/month**

**Winner depends on quality needs:** Runway for cinematic/controlled motion, Seedance for character consistency at scale

---

### Recommended Strategy

**For AI content pipelines at scale:**

1. **Use Seedance for routine AI influencer clips** — the cost advantage is significant at volume
2. **Use Runway selectively** — when you need precise motion control or cinematic quality for a hero video or ad
3. **Don't use either for B-roll** — that's what Pexels free API is for
4. **Budget rule of thumb:** Allocate AI video budget to the *first 3 seconds* of your video (the hook). That's what stops the scroll. The rest can be Remotion + Pexels.
